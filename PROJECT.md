# India Weather Intelligence Platform

## Objective

Provide near real-time weather and business continuity intelligence for India.

## Users

- Business Continuity Teams
- Corporate Management
- Regional Operations

## Primary Goals

- Monitor severe weather
- Track disruptions
- Generate executive summaries
- Notify on important changes

## Guiding Principle

Government data provides facts.
Business intelligence provides decisions.

## Long Term Sources

Tier 1
- Sachet
- IMD

Tier 2
- Traffic
- Metro
- Railways
- Flights

Tier 3
- News
- Social Media

## Dashboard Philosophy

Management should understand:

- What happened?
- Where?
- Business impact?
- Recommended action?

within 30 seconds.
